About Elise

Strategy built on
truth, not performance.

After a decade in private wealth management at two of London's oldest firms, I left with one realisation: the women earning the most were often building the least.

Not from lack of intelligence. Not from lack of ambition. From never being given a framework that put their financial future first — rather than the interests of an institution.

I built Hartwell Coaching to change that. My work is part financial strategy, part mindset architecture — because knowing what to do and actually doing it are two entirely different problems.
